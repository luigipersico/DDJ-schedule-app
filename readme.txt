🇨🇭 Welcome to the SPC DDJ Scheduling Portal 2.0

Say goodbye to messy spreadsheets and endless email chains. We have officially upgraded the DDJ scheduling process to a fully automated, cloud-based web application.
This new system is designed to do three things: make updating your availability frictionless, keep the lab schedule 100% transparent, and guarantee that shift distribution is mathematically fair for everyone.
Here is everything you need to know about how to use the new portal.

1. Finding Your Profile

There are no passwords or accounts to create. Simply open the web link and select your name from the dropdown menu.
If you are a new PhD: Select "I am a NEW PhD (Add me)", type your full name, and hit Enter. The system will instantly generate a new profile for you.


2. Setting Your Availability

Once you select your name, you will be on the 📝 My Availability tab. This is your personal control center.
Typical Weekly Availability: Check the boxes for the mornings (AM) and afternoons (PM) you are generally free.
The "Two-Slot Minimum" Rule: To ensure we always have enough coverage, the system requires you to be available for at least two slots between Tuesday and Friday. If you try to select fewer, the system will reject your save attempt! (If you have a legitimate, long-term conflict, speak with a DDJ ninja).
Specific Away Dates: Going to a conference? Taking a holiday? Add your dates in the "Away Dates" section with the reason. The algorithm will automatically skip you for those specific days. (Note: Past away dates are automatically cleaned up from your profile).


3. How the "Fairness Algorithm" Works

You might notice a Fairness Ratio on your sidebar.
The algorithm calculates your ratio by dividing your Total Completed Shifts by your Active Months in the SPC.
When the app assigns a shift, it looks at everyone who is available that day and gives the shift to the person with the lowest Fairness Ratio.
This ensures that over the course of your PhD, nobody is doing more or less than their fair share.

4. Your Personal Dashboard & Stats

When you load your profile, check out the sidebar on the left. It tracks your exact contributions to the lab:
Your Status: See instantly if you are active in the rotation, or if you are marked as "Inactive".
Upcoming Absences: A clean list of your upcoming registered holidays and conferences so you know your dates were saved correctly.
Personalized Visual Calendar: Once the monthly schedule is published, your sidebar will show a color-coded calendar. Bank holidays are blue, TCV Maintenance days are red, and if you have a shift, your initials will light up in bold yellow.

5. The Lab Historical Dashboard

Curious about who is on shift this week, or need to check a past month? Click the 📅 DDJ Historical Dashboard tab at the top. You can browse through the entire history of published schedules.
